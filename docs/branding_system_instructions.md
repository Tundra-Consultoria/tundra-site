# Visual Identity Manual - Tundra Consultoria

## 1. Brand Essence and Concept
* [cite_start]Tundra Consultoria is a brand that merges modern, minimalist, straight, and sans-serif typographic traits with a strong, detailed, and naturalistic visual icon (the mountain)[cite: 194].
* [cite_start]This duality is derived from the company's core values: offering cutting-edge technology services, relying on solid methodologies, and interacting with the client in a transparent manner[cite: 195].
* [cite_start]The reference is direct: the tundra biome inspired the logo's icon and the color palette[cite: 196]. [cite_start]This name was also adopted for its symbolic appeal: it refers to a cold landscape of adverse conditions, where one must adapt quickly to survive[cite: 197].
* [cite_start]Therefore, Tundra resembles us, since effort, resilience, and rapid adaptation are the north star for transforming complex scenarios into efficient results[cite: 198].

## 2. Visual Signature (Logotype)
* [cite_start]**Main Composition:** The primary visual signature consists of the mountain symbol, a straight separating bar, and the nominative block "Tundra Consultoria"[cite: 199].
* [cite_start]**Logo Typographic Configuration:** Within the visual signature's nominative block, the word "Tundra" is configured in Bold and "Consultoria" in Regular[cite: 200].
* [cite_start]**Permitted Variations:** Simplified variations are allowed without the horizontal bar and without the symbol, as well as the use of the isolated icon[cite: 201].
* [cite_start]**Color Applications:** * It is recommended to apply the visual signature strictly in a monochromatic version (Black or White)[cite: 202].
  * [cite_start]Examples of the logo's application over Red and Green backgrounds can be found within the brand guidelines[cite: 203].
  * [cite_start]Always check the contrast between colors to ensure legibility[cite: 204].
* [cite_start]**Watermark:** Note that the background of the pages in this handbook features the symbol applied as a watermark with low opacity[cite: 205].

## 4. Typographic System
[cite_start]The typographic system of Tundra Consultoria adopts the DM Sans font family, utilizing the ExtraLight, Regular, SemiBold, and Bold variations[cite: 214].

### Main Family: DM Sans
* [cite_start]**Reading Typography (Body Text):** The ExtraLight weight is recommended, and substitution with the Regular weight is permitted[cite: 215].
* [cite_start]**Titles and Highlight Statements:** These must be applied in SemiBold or Bold[cite: 216].
* [cite_start]**Subtitles:** The Regular weight is reserved for subtitles[cite: 217].

### Complementary Family: Unna
[cite_start]Additionally, the serif font Unna is introduced as an element of editorial contrast[cite: 218].
* [cite_start]**Permitted Applications:** It is exclusively reserved for specific details, such as highlighted quotes, section numbers, or graphic elements of a documentary nature[cite: 219].
* [cite_start]**Restrictions:** The use of Unna must be restricted to applications that require an extra layer of sophistication and legibility in short bodies[cite: 220]. [cite_start]It must be avoided in long reading texts to preserve the visual hierarchy established by the DM Sans family[cite: 221].
